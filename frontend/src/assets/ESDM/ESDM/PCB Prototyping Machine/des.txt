Technical Specifications
Working Area: e.g., 229mm x 305mm (9" x 12")
Minimum Trace/Space: e.g., 0.1mm / 4 mil
Drilling Speed: Up to e.g., 120 strokes/min
File Compatibility: Standard Gerber (RS-274X), Excellon Drill, and DXF files.
Supported Materials: FR4, FR1, CEM1, G-10, Polyimide, and PTFE (RT/duroid).
Connectivity: USB 2.0 with galvanic isolation.

Electronics R&D Labs: Rapidly validating circuit theories and embedded systems.
Aerospace & Defense: Keeping sensitive intellectual property (IP) entirely in-house.
Universities: Teaching PCB design and fabrication in a clean, hands-on environment.
Medical Startups: Fast prototyping of imaging and diagnostic equipment controls.
